Crystal Counter

Crystals

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals on the side. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching you total score to the random number. You lose the game if you go over the random number.
The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of the crystals.
Good luck!
